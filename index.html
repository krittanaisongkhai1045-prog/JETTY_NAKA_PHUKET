About Thanawat Engineering Phuket

Thanawat Engineering Phuket Limited Partnership is a marine engineering company specializing in marine works, steel structures, and coastal infrastructure. Established in 1983 in Phuket, the company has over 40 years of experience in the industry.

Currently managed by Mr. Jirawat Kuramakanok, Thanawat Engineering Phuket is trusted by government agencies, local authorities, and private clients nationwide, and remains committed to providing reliable and sustainable marine engineering solutions.
