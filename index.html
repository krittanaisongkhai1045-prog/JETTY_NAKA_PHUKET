About Thanawat Engineering Phuket Limited Partnership

Thanawat Engineering Phuket is a marine engineering company specializing in marine works, steel structures, and coastal infrastructure. Established in 1983 in Phuket, the company has over 40 years of experience in the industry.

The company provides services in the construction, repair, and improvement of marine structures, including jetties, pontoons, floating structures, mooring systems, marine piling works, as well as steel structures and custom fabrication. With extensive experience in real marine environments, the company delivers strong, safe, and practical solutions suited for long-term use.

Currently managed by Mr. Jirawat Kuramakanok, Thanawat Engineering Phuket is trusted by government agencies, local authorities, and private clients nationwide, and remains committed to providing reliable and sustainable marine engineering solutions.
